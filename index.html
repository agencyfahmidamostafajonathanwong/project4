Welcome to Yugi-Oh Duel Monsters!

Instructions

  • You start the game with 2000 Lifepoints.
  • You can find them marked by LP in the bottom left corner.
  • The opponent's life points can be found in the top left corner.
  • To select a monster click on one of your cards on the bottom half of the game board.
  • Your monster's attack power will appear in the lower-right corner
  • To select an opponent's monster to attack, click on one of the opponent's cards
  • Your opponent's monster's attack appears in the top right corner.
  • You may only defeat enemy monsters with a lower attack power than your own.
  • When you are ready to attack, click the attack button.
  • To de-select cards at any time, click on cancel.
  • When a player reaches 0 lifepoints they lose!

GAME OVER!
